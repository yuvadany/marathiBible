 1 Kabaka anaasanyukiranga amaanyi go, ai Mukama; N'obulokozi bwo ng'anaabujagulizanga nnyo!
 2 Omuwadde omutima gwe kye gwagala, So tomummye emimwa gye kye gisabye.
 (Seera) 3 Kubanga omusisinkanye n'eby'omukisa eby'obulungi: Omuteekako ku mutwe gwe engule ey'ezaabu ennungi.
 4 Yakusaba obulamu, n'obumuwa; Era n'ennaku ennyingi ez'emirembe n'emirembe.
 5 Ekitiibwa kye kingi mu bulokozi bwo: Ekitiibwa n'obukulu obimussaako.
 6 Kubanga omuteekawo okuba omukisa omungi emirembe gyonna: Omusanyusa essanyu mu maaso go.
 7 Kubanga kabaka yeesiga Mukama, N'olw'ekisa ky'oyo ali waggulu ennyo taasagaasaganenga.
 8 Omukono gwo gulituuka ku balabe bo bonna: Omukono gwo ogwa ddyo gulituuka ku abo abakukyawa.
 9 Olibafaananya ng'ekikoomi ekyaka mu biro eby'obusungu bwo.
 Mukama alibamira mu kiruyi kye, N'omuliro gulibalya.
 10 Ebibala byabwe olibizikiriza ku nsi, N'ezzadde lyabwe mu baana b'abantu.
 11 Kubanga baakuteeseza obubi: Baasala olukwe, lwe bataayinza kukola.
 12 Kubanga olibazza ennyuma, Oliteekateeka engoye zo ez'omutego ku maaso gaabwe.
 13 Ogulumizibwenga, ai Mukama, mu maanyi go: Tulyoke tuyimbenga tutenderezenga obuyinza bwo.
