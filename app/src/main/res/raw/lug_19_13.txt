 1 Olituusa wa, ai Mukama, okunneerabiranga emirembe gyonna? Olituusa wa okunkisanga amaaso go? 2 Ndituusa wa okuteesanga mu mmeeme yange, Nga ndaba ennaku mu mutima gwange obudde okuziba? Alituusa wa omulabe wange okugulumizibwanga ku nze? 3 Lowooza onziremu, ai Mukama Katonda wange: Yakira amaaso gange, nneme okwebaka okufa; 4 Omulabe wange alemenga okwogera nti Mmuwangudde: Abankyawa balemenga okujaguza nga nsagaasagana.
 5 Naye nneesize okusaasira kwo; Omutima gwange gunaasanyukiranga obulokozi bwo: 6 Naayimbiranga Mukama, Kubanga ankoledde obulungi obungi.
