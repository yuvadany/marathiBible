 1 Mukama bw'atazimba nnyumba, Abagizimba bakolera bwereere: Mukama bw'atakuuma kibuga, Omukuumi atunuulirira bwereere.
 2 Mukolera bwereere bwe mukeera okugolokoka, era bwe mulwawo ennyo okwebaka; Era bwe mulya emmere ey'okutegana: Kubanga bw'abawa bw'atyo otulo abaagalwa be.
 3 Laba, abaana bwe busika bwa Mukama: N'ebibala eby'olubuto ye mpeera ye.
 4 Ng'obusaale bwe buli mu mukono gw'omuzira, Abaana ab'omu buvubuka bwe bali bwe batyo.
 5 Alina omukisa omuntu omufuko gwe bwe gujjula abo: Tebaakwatibwenga nsonyi, Bwe banaayogereranga n'abalabe baabwe mu mulyango.
